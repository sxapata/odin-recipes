Honey Soy Salmon

Pan fried salmon cooked in honey soy sauce

Salmon is salted and peppered to taste. Cooked under medium heat on both sides. Sauce is added towards the end and is basted over the salmon.

Ingredients

  • Salmon 2 fillets
  • Soy sauce 2tbsp
  • Honey 2tbsp
  • Oyster sauce 1tbsp
  • Worcestershire sauce 1tbsp
  • White wine vingar 1tbsp
  • Mirin 1tbsp

Steps

  1. Pat salmon dry. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Fry salmon pink side up for about 5 minutes on medium heat. Flip when salmon flips easily from pan.
  3. Add sauce to salmon and cook through for about 4-6 minutes.
