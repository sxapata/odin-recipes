Breakfast Porridge

Fast and easy breakfast porridge

Oatmeal prepared with your choice of milk. Topped with fruits, seeds, and nuts.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal 1cup
  • Soy milk (or any other kind of milk) 1cup
  • Chia seeds 1tbsp
  • Hemp seeds 1tbsp
  • Coca powder 1/2tbsp
  • Banana 1

Steps

  1. Pour milk onto oats and place under low heat for 3 mins.
  2. Remove from heat and add chia seeds, hemp seeds, coca powder, and banana.
